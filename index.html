ABOUT

Ivy Mae Photography is a new photography business of Ivy Chapel. She has recently graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Photography and Illustration, and is keen to put her skill set to work. Ivy has worked on some minor freelance projects for friends and family, but hopes to expand her client base outwards and work on more professional projects. Ivy's interest lies predominantly in fashion and editorial photography, however she also has some interest in portraiture.
